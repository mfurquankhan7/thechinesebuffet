Restaurants

The ninth restaurant in our ever growing, exciting chain of Chinese Buffet Restaurants opened its doors on 10 October 2014 at 61-63 Church Street, Blackpool, just round the corner from the famous Blackpool Tower.

The restaurant is 9000 square feet and seats 300 diners who can enjoy a range of countless dishes and boasts a fantastic Teppanyaki theatre style open kitchen, Dim Sum bar and Sushi bar. A wide range of desserts is complemented by the very popular ice cream machine and chocolate fountain.

This restaurant continues to demonstrate THE Chinese Buffets reputation for providing high quality authentic Chinese Food in high quality surroundings and its doors are open all year round, catering for parties large and small, with a number of luxurious VIP booths.

Who are We

The Chinese Buffet is a chain of restaurants in the North, West and East of England, offering a range of Oriental cuisine. Eleven restaurants have already been established, and now still expanding its operations. It has ambitious plans and the vision is to build a brand that is recognised nationwide.

The business model employed by The Chinese Buffet is both competitive and profitable. The company understands current food culture and market trends, and delivers an experience that exceeds customer expectations.

The Chinese Buffet is unique in its belief that it can shape culture and convention through its exemplary service and its remarkable food.

Our Growth Story

Aim

Ultimately, our aim is to build The Chinese Buffet into an national brand. We are primarily looking at growth in the north west of UK, followed by expansion into the rest of UK.

Expansion plan
  • 2016- TCB production line. Centralise production
  • 2018- 20 restaurants with average of 250 seats capacity within 100 miles from Bolton, Lancashire
  • 2020- Franchise THE Chinese Buffet brand nationwide
Investment in technology and social media

Investing in technology is key to the success of the business. The Chinese Buffet is investing into marketing and management systems. ‘Auto schedule marketing’ tools such as SMS are used for market research and promotional activities. One campaign, ‘Chinese Wednesday’ campaign has demonstrated how the implementation of this technology has significantly increased business, with a 300% growth in revenue since its inception. THE Chinese buffet Facebook has grown from 4000 followers to 100,000 followers in 20 months.

Affordability

The Chinese Buffet has a competitive pricing policy, pitching its all-you-can-enjoy buffet at a third less than the price of a typical a la carte restaurant meal; but with a dining experience that is double the fun. Customers appreciate the attractive pricing and return again and again for an informal and enjoyable dining experience.

Customer satisfaction

The Chinese Buffet seeks to make a difference. There is a culture of high moral standards and the company-wide goal is to deliver customer satisfaction. All staff are trained to focus solely on delivering outstanding service. The carefully designed menu, allied to this committed service has resulted in a 6% increase in new customers every month for the past three years of operation. To date, the restaurants have served 20,000 customers weekly.

Employee retention

Today’s world is a competitive place for employers, particularly in the hospitality trade. Success depends on employing the very best people at all levels. By educating, training and supporting staff, The Chinese Buffet is able to increase job satisfaction and loyalty and also create a high level of employee morale. It is testament to the success of this approach that 80% of the senior staff have been with the company for more than three years.

Team

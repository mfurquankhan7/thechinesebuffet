Bookings:

  • When making a group booking of 6 people or more we require a deposit of 25% perperson of the meal price for that particular session.
  • The deposit must be paid no later than 24 hours before your booking and for thetotal number of people booked for.
  • The deposit will secure the table reservation for the party for up to 30minutes after the agreed booking time. Thereafter the booking is null and void.However we will endeavour to accommodate you as and when your party arrives ata new time.
  • In the event of your party running late it is the responsibility of you toinform the restaurant, so that we can make arrangements to find you a new tableas soon as we can.
  • If you haven’t paid a deposit your table reservation is held for a maximum of15 minutes only, after this time your reservation will be null and void.However we will endeavour to seat you as soon as we can, once the majority ofthe party has arrived.
  • The party will only be seated once the majority of people have arrived.
  • Once seated your table is available to you for a maximum of 1 hour and 45minutes during our busy times. This is at the management’s discretion and doneon a case by case basis.
  • All guests sitting in the restaurant are considered to be eating - we do notallow those not eating a table just for drinking. Therefore anyone sat in therestaurant will be charged for a meal at that daily session rate.
  • If no deposit has been received (due to insufficient time etc) and the group isof 6 people or more the table will be held for a maximum of 15 minutes only.After which the reservation is null and void. However we will endeavour to seatthe party as soon as the majority of the party have arrived.

Refunds:

  • Deposit refunds will only be considered if cancellation is made 24 hours ormore before the booking.
  • Refunds will not be considered in part payment if not all those booked for turnup, unless over 24 hours notice is given of the amended numbers.
  • THE Chinese buffet holds the right to alter the terms and conditions of thedeposit / refund / booking policy without prior notice.

Timings:

  • We operate a maximum of 1 hour 45 minutes seating policy during our busysessions such as (Friday, Saturday, Christmas, Valentine’s Day, Bank Holidaysand Mother /Father Days)
  • The 1 hour 45 minute policy will start from the time you are seated. Where themajority of the party have been seated the 1 hour 45 minute policy will runfrom this time and not when the last of the party arrives.
  • The last entry for the restaurant is 30 minutes prior to closing.
  • The food on the buffet will cease to be replenished at this time and the foodwill start to be brought in 15 minutes prior to closing.
  • At the discretion of the management small dishes of food will be producedduring the 30 minutes before closing time.
  • Where a party has made no reservation we cannot guarantee a table for the full1 hour 45 minutes. We will advise you of what availability we have and bysitting down you acknowledge that this is acceptable.
